About the Project

MCA Final Year Project — Urban Traffic Intelligence and Congestion Prediction System

Problem Statement

Urban traffic congestion is one of the most critical challenges faced by modern cities in India and around the world. Rapid urbanization, population growth, and an increase in the number of vehicles have led to severe traffic bottlenecks, especially during peak hours. This results in:

  • Excessive travel delays ranging from 30 minutes to over 2 hours in metro cities
  • Increased fuel consumption and carbon emissions contributing to pollution
  • Higher accident rates in congested areas due to driver frustration
  • Economic losses estimated at thousands of crores annually due to time and fuel wastage
  • Inadequate real-time traffic information for commuters to make informed route decisions

Existing traffic management systems rely on outdated manual methods and lack predictive capability, making it impossible to proactively manage congestion before it occurs.

Proposed System

The Urban Traffic Intelligence and Congestion Prediction System is a full-stack AI-powered web application designed to solve these challenges. The system:

Predicts Congestion

Uses a trained Random Forest ML model to classify traffic as Low, Medium, or High based on 8 input parameters.

Suggests Alternate Routes

Intelligently recommends safer and faster alternate routes based on congestion and weather conditions.

Generates Real-time Alerts

Automatically raises high congestion alerts stored in the database for immediate action.

Provides Analytics

Integrates Power BI dashboards and Chart.js for visual analysis of traffic patterns and trends.
